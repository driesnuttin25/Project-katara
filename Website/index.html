About Our Plant Monitoring System

Welcome to Project Katara! This innovative platform allows you to keep track of your plants' health remotely. Using advanced sensors and data analytics, we provide real-time updates on your plant's temperature, moisture levels, and soil ion percentages.

Our system is designed to make plant care easy and accessible. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting out, our tools can help you maintain the perfect environment for your plants.

This project was designed by me, Dries Nuttin. It was designed for a school project that turned out to take a lot longer than I had anticipated. But hey, now we're here.
